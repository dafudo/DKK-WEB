Services of Dawid Karol Kołodziej

mgr Dawid Karol Kołodziej is a mental health specialist, psychologist, pedagogue, therapist, business and career advisor. With over 8 years of experience in the industry, he offers a wide range of services for individuals and companies who want to improve their well-being, develop their skills and achieve their goals.

Individual therapy or counseling

If you are looking for professional help in solving emotional, personal or professional problems, mgr Dawid Karol Kołodziej invites you to individual sessions of therapy or counseling. Depending on your needs and preferences, you can choose from one of the following methods:

  • CBT therapy - cognitive-behavioral therapy that helps you change negative thoughts and behaviors that make your life difficult.
  • PDT therapy - psychodynamic therapy that helps you understand and resolve deep-rooted conflicts and traumas from the past.
  • Psychoanalytic therapy - therapy based on Freud’s theory that helps you discover and heal unconscious motives and desires that affect your behavior and relationships.
  • Hypnotherapy - therapy using hypnosis that helps you reduce stress, anxiety and pain and increase your self-confidence and motivation.

Each session lasts about 50 minutes and costs from 150 AUD. Sessions take place in the office in Bolesławiec or online via Skype or Zoom.

Socioterapy

If you want to improve your relationships with other people, learn how to communicate and cooperate better and feel part of a community, mgr Dawid Karol Kołodziej invites you to socioterapy. Socioterapy is a form of group therapy that uses various methods and techniques, such as:

  • Games and integration activities - activities aimed at establishing contact and building trust among participants.
  • Dramatherapy - activities using theater and improvisation to express emotions and create life scenarios.
  • Art therapy - activities using art and creativity to explore your own identity and imagination.
  • Music therapy - activities using music and sound to relax and harmonize with others.

Each session lasts 90 minutes and costs from 150 AUD per person. Sessions take place in groups of 5 to 10 people in a workshop room in Wrocław or Legnica.

Trainings and lectures

If you want to raise your qualifications or those of your employees, mgr Dawid Karol Kołodziej offers trainings and lectures in the field of psychology, pedagogy and business. You can choose from the following topics:

  • Building structures and managing a company - how to create an effective organization that fulfills its mission and vision and ensures customer and employee satisfaction.
  • Project management - how to plan, organize and control the course of a project and how to deal with risk, changes and problems.
  • Resolving intra-company conflicts - how to recognize the sources of conflicts at work and how to effectively resolve them using negotiation techniques and mediation.
  • Soft skills - how to develop social and emotional competencies, such as communication, cooperation, assertiveness, empathy or time management.

Each training or lecture lasts from 2 to 4 hours and costs from 400 AUD per training unit. Trainings and lectures can take place at the company’s headquarters or online via Skype or Zoom.
