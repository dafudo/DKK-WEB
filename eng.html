Biography

Dawid Karol Kołodziej is a versatile specialist in the field of mental health, psychology, pedagogy, and business and career counseling. His dynamic personality and rich experience in working with people make him often invited to participate in projects related to personal development, education, therapy, and management.

He graduated from master’s studies in psychology with a forensic specialty and social sciences, and also has specializations in the field of resocialization, career counseling, and personal counseling. For over 8 years Dawid has been the head of therapeutic clinics in several cities, where he helps people solve various problems, both emotional and professional. His approach is based on knowledge from various fields of science, which allows for effective and professional approach to each patient.

Dawid is the President of the Main Board of the Psychopedagogical Society and Hobweek sp. z o.o. He is also a laureate of many awards and distinctions for his skills in the field of education, therapy, and management, among others the title of “Microsoft Innovative Educator Expert” and the award named after Jan Karski.

As a respected specialist, Dawid is often invited to participate in various social projects and to cooperate with companies and business representatives. He supports many people in developing their professional career and achieving their goals through individual or group sessions, which take place both physically and online.

Dawid is also a well-known media expert, regularly giving interviews on television and radio, where he shares his knowledge and experience.

He is married to Aleksandra Górczyńska-Kołodziej, with whom he has two wonderful children - Karol and Amelia. In his free time Dawid loves to travel and discover new cultures, as well as draw inspiration from pop culture, such as movies, psychological series or RPG games, both in table-top and video versions. He is also a fan of riding a chopper motorcycle and amateurly deals with forging swords and knives from hardened steel, which allows him to develop his interests and passions. His versatile interests and rich experience in working with people make him a valuable and inspiring person for anyone who seeks support in personal and professional development.
